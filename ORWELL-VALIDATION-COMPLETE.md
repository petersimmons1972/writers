## Lessons Learned (Orwell-Specific)

### What Worked Exceptionally Well

1. **Numbered taxonomy as signature marker**
   - Most immediately recognizable Orwell pattern (besides self-incrimination)
   - Zero occurrences in Pyle/Murrow, 58% in Orwell corpus
   - Functional (intellectual control) not decorative

2. **Self-incriminating honesty vs. Pyle's humble ignorance**
   - Pyle: "I don't know much" (frequent, builds intimacy)
   - Orwell: "I will admit my bias" (strategic, builds credibility)
   - Different functions, both involve admission

3. **Bureaucratic parallel technique**
   - Classic Orwell: technical system → political structure
   - Multi-agent AI → organizational dysfunction
   - Transfers perfectly to modern domains

4. **Concrete → abstract escalation**
   - Strongest Orwell pattern (100% corpus frequency)
   - Clearly distinguishes from Pyle (shows only) and Murrow (analyzes)
   - Physical observation → diagnosis → political generalization

**Not just formality level:**
- Pyle (Grade 8-9) vs. Murrow (Grade 11-12) vs. Orwell (Grade 10-12)
- Orwell is NOT between them, it's a DIFFERENT AXIS

**Structural distinction:**
- Pyle = MUSIC (rhythmic variety)
- Murrow = ARCHITECTURE (logical blocks)
- Orwell = DIAGNOSIS (systematic analysis)

**Perspective distinction:**
- Pyle = Intimate witness (watching WITH reader)
