### Accomplishments

**Deliverable**: `~/projects/writers/corpus/edward-murrow/guidelines.md` (954 lines)

**Guidelines Structure**:

**Tier 1: Non-Negotiables (8 mandatory rules)**
1. Geographic/temporal grounding (first 15 words)
2. Formal witness voice (Grade 11-12 reading level)
3. Zero one-sentence paragraphs (fundamental boundary)
4. Building rhythm - ARCHITECTURE not music
5. Strategic "you" usage (1-2 per 100 words, not constant)
6. Signature phrases ("This is London", "Good night and good luck")
7. Admitted inadequacy of language ("There are no words")
8. Formalized or devastating endings (never casual)

**Tier 2: Strong Patterns (12 guidelines, target 70%+ compliance)**
1. 17-word average sentence length (formality through length)
2. 4-5 sentences per paragraph (logical units)
3. Temporal/spatial transitions (not logical connectives)
4. Parallel structure for emphasis (rhetorical parallelism)
5. "I" as witness thread (2-3 per 100 words)
6. Physical observation dominates (65% of content)
7. Chronological narrative structure (60% of samples)
8. Precision tools not hedges (qualifiers for accuracy)
9. Controlled authority tone (never casual, never preachy)
10. Concrete > abstract vocabulary
11. Technical detail when measurable
12. Conflict shown but rarely resolved

**Tier 3: Contextual Flourishes (8 techniques, use 1-2 when appropriate)**
1. "There are no words" technique (20% of samples - use sparingly)
2. Buchenwald-style overt anger (rare, only for overwhelming injustice)
3. Ironic understatement ("Young storm troopers... singing")
4. Formal courtesy before difficult content ("Permit me to tell you")
5. Rhetorical questions (used strategically, not frequently)
6. Extended metaphor (rare, purposeful)
7. Catalog accumulation (building to overwhelming scale)
8. Signature sign-off adaptation (context-appropriate formality)

**Actionable Components**:

1. **Writing Checklist** (3-stage validation)
   - Pre-flight: 8 Tier 1 items (must hit ALL)
   - Strong patterns: 12 Tier 2 items (hit 8+/12 minimum)
   - Flourishes: 8 Tier 3 items (use 1-2 maximum, not all)

2. **Anti-Pattern Verification** (Zero-tolerance items)
   - Tier 1 violations (ANY = instant fail): One-sentence paragraphs, casual slang, summary conclusions
   - Tier 2 violations (2+ = probably fail): Overly rhythmic variety, constant "you" immersion, hedging language
   - Tier 3 violations (awareness items): Generic broadcast clichés, false expertise, unnecessary metaphor

3. **Validation Rubric** (100-point scoring system)
   - Tier 1: 40 points (5 per non-negotiable, must score 36+/40)
   - Tier 2: 40 points (3-4 per strong pattern, must score 32+/40)
   - Tier 3: 20 points (2-3 per appropriate flourish, bonus)
   - Passing: 80+/100 overall, Tier 1 ≥36, Tier 2 ≥32

4. **Traceability Matrix**
   - Every guideline maps to pattern analysis section
   - Frequency data included (e.g., "40% of openings" → "Use geographic grounding")
   - Evidence cited from specific samples
   - Comparison notes to Pyle where relevant

5. **Common Mistakes to Avoid** (8 critical errors documented)
   - Using one-sentence paragraphs (instant Pyle marker, not Murrow)
   - Casual conversational tone (Grade 8 instead of Grade 11-12)
   - Constant "you" immersion (Pyle technique, not Murrow's strategic use)
   - Rhythmic variety for variety's sake (music vs. architecture)
   - Summary conclusions or moral lessons (neither writer does this)
   - Hedging when precision possible (Murrow uses qualifiers for accuracy)
   - Missing geographic/temporal grounding (Murrow's universal opening)
   - Forgetting signature phrases (recognition markers)
