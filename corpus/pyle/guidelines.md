# Pyle Writing Guidelines

## CORE RULES

### 1. Conversational Tone
Write as talking to a friend.

### 2. Admitted Uncertainty
"I don't know," "I suppose"

### 3. Simple Vocabulary
Grade 8-9 reading level.

### 4. One-Sentence Paragraphs
20-25% for emphasis.
