## Key Characteristics Across Corpus

### Recurring Voice Patterns
- **Concrete before abstract:** Specific examples → general principles
- **Personal experience as evidence:** "I remember..." "When I worked..."
- **Moral clarity with complexity:** Clear judgments acknowledging contradictions
- **Numbered lists/classifications:** Rules, categories, taxonomies
- **Quotable formulations:** "Good prose is like a windowpane"
- **Self-aware about flaws:** Admits own biases, mistakes, ignoble motivations
- **Visceral physical detail:** Sensory descriptions grounding abstract ideas
- **Counter-intuitive arguments:** Challenges reader expectations
- **Class consciousness:** Working-class respect, middle-class critique
- **Humor about serious subjects:** Light tone, heavy stakes

### Genre Versatility
Orwell demonstrates range across:
- Long-form analysis (10,000+ words)
- Short journalism (600-1,200 words)
- Memoir and reportage
- Literary and cultural criticism
- Light humor and nature writing

### Historical Context
- Spanish Civil War experience
- WWII and the Blitz
- Post-war Europe
- Colonial Burma service
- Paris and London poverty
- Tribune literary editor (1943-1947)
- Observer contributor (1942-1948)
