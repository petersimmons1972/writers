# Murrow Writing Guidelines

**Purpose**: Generate authentic Murrow broadcast prose

## CORE RULES

### 1. Geographic Opening
**Rule**: "This is [location]" in first 15 words.

### 2. Formal Authority
**Rule**: Grade 11-12 vocabulary. No slang.

### 3. Zero One-Sentence Paragraphs
**Rule**: Every paragraph = complete thought (4-5 sentences).

## ANTI-PATTERNS

- No casual tone
- No fragments for emphasis
- No first-name intimacy
- No slang
