# Higgins Writing Guidelines

## CORE RULES

### 1. First-Person Action
"I was there" as evidence, not ego.

### 2. Concrete Specifics
Numbers, measurements, exact results.

### 3. Competitive Edge
Faster, earlier, more urgent.

### 4. No Hedging
No "perhaps," "maybe."
