## Technical Recommendations

### To Push Score to 90+

1. **Add 1-2 more colons** per 1000 words (currently 2.46, target 3.0+)
   - Look for "which is" or "because" - replace with colon structure

2. **Increase short sentence ratio** to 15-20% (currently 12%)
   - After long sentences, add punchy 3-5 word declarations
   - Example: "The coffee is cold." "I guessed wrong." "Perhaps that is the point."

3. **Include one third-person self-reference**
   - Example: "the writer who photographs lunch to think about training data"
   - Example: "she who obsesses over water systems and training datasets"

### Maintaining Authenticity

**DO**:
- Trust the concrete → abstract pattern religiously
- Admit uncertainty generously (but not excessively)
- Question abstractions before using them
- Let time be ambiguous when memory is unclear
- Choose precise words over accessible words

**DON'T**:
- Dramatize emotions (detachment, not coldness)
- Claim certainty you don't have
- Follow strict chronology when theme resists it
- Use common words when exact words exist
- Resolve philosophical questions with neat conclusions
